Pasta alla Norma

Description

Pasta alla Norma - Currently one of my favourite Summer dishes!

It is made from fried eggplant in a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • 1 large eggplant
  • Freh tomatoes or 1 can tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • 1 large red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves (or more)
  • Oil, salt, pepper, herbs
  • For garnish: Yeast flakes and fresh basil

Steps

  1. Cut the augbergine in small cubes, let them rest in a bowl with a genoures amount of salt
  2. Cut the onion, garlic cloves and fresh tomatoes
  3. Rinse the augbergines with water an pad dry
  4. Fry until golden brown and set aside
  5. Fry the onion and garlic until golden
  6. Add tomato paste and let it brown a little
  7. Then add the aubergine, fry briefly and deglace with the tomatoes/li>
  8. Season the sauce with salt, pepper and herbs
  9. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes and cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet
  10. Mix the pasta with the sauce and garnish with yeast flakes and fresh basil
