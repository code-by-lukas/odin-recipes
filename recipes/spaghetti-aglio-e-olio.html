Spaghetti aglio e olio

Description

Another simple, but very tasty dish made of only three ingredients

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt, pepper
  • Yeast flakes for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan to low medium heat
  2. Slice the garlic
  3. Add a generous amount of oil to the pan
  4. Add garlic and let it simmer on low heat
  5. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until almost done
  6. Drain the pasta, save some of the cooking water
  7. Add pasta to the pan an finish cooking
  8. Add some pasta water to thicken it
  9. Serve with pepper and yeast flakes
